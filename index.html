About The Moon Talkers

We are here to share views of our sky with the North and Central Florida community. The Moon, the Sun (with a safety filter), Saturn, Jupiter, and more can be seen through one of our telescopes. Check out our events page for information about where we will be next. If you are interested in hosting a telescope pop-up, please reach out via the contact page.

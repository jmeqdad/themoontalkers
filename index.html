Tools of the trade

Polaroid and Instax Cameras are used, along with a Celestron Telescope, to create instant film photographs of the Sun and Moon.

Moon shot on Polaroid

Telescope tucked away in the garden at Lamplight AVL

In 2025, I participated in my first residency at Lamplight AVL, a local non-profil organization that provides support to experimental artists. During the residency residency I photographed over 100 moons and suns on instant film. To see more information about my experience, please visit my page on Lamplight AVL.
